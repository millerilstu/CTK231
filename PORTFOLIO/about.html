About The Artist

Mason Shrike is a digital illustrator based in Central Illinois and a Creative Technologies student at Illinois State University. He specializes in figure drawing, character design, and storyboarding, creating expressive visuals that highlight strong characters and clear narrative moments. He primarily works in Procreate and Adobe Creative Cloud.

Mason is currently open to illustration commissions, internships, and collaborative projects. For inquiries, he can be reached at Masonshrike01@gmail.com, and you can connect with him on Instagram and Bluesky @Masonshrike.
